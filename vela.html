My Work

Surfing You Weather App

Surfing You is an app that provides easy to understand reports and stats for water sports aficionado for trip planning.

Why Surfing You?

Weather reports are crucial in planning safe days on the water, but most sources out there can be tough to understand, especially for those who are new. Surfing You provides an app that can help water sport aficionados have and stay safe.
